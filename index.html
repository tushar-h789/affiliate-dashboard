Commissions are calculated weekly (Sunday to Saturday) and paid out every Tuesday. Minimum payout threshold is $50. Payments are made via the same method as your account deposits.

Referrals are tracked using cookies (30-day expiry) and referral codes (permanent attribution). If a user clicks your link and signs up within 30 days, you get credit even without using your code.

Self-referrals, fake accounts, and duplicate accounts are strictly prohibited. Spamming or using misleading advertising may result in account suspension. All referred users must be 18+ and comply with local gambling laws.

Geographic Restrictions

Referral program is available in all supported countries. However, commission rates may vary by region due to local regulations. Check your account dashboard for region-specific rates.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes regarding referral attribution or commission calculations must be reported within 7 days. Our support team will investigate and resolve issues within 5 business days.
